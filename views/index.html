Who We Are

Global business engineering

The concept of CoWork was born from the firsthand challenges experienced by a group of students at Via University College in securing study-relevant jobs. This spark ignited CoWork's inception, evolving it into a progressively larger project led by four handsome fellows studying Global Business Engineering.
